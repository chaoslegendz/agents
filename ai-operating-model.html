OSFI / DATA RESIDENCY NOTE
No customer data, counterparty data, or regulatory position data in AI prompts via browser tools. Enterprise tools (Copilot, M365) operate within bank tenant boundary. Claude browser use: architecture, templates, reasoning — not live data. Validate any AI output before it enters regulated workflows (SA-CCR, CVA, FRTB).

The chain converts AI from additive (each role uses AI independently) to multiplicative (each step builds on the last). The artifact is the baton.

FOUNDING PRINCIPLE
"Central AI gives you the map. Your champion knows the terrain."
What a Champion Is
· Embedded within their role (BSA, Dev, QA, Prod)
· Primary contact for AI questions on the team
· Pilots new patterns before team-wide rollout
· Runs retros and surfaces what's working / not
What a Champion Is Not
· A prompt engineer or dedicated AI role
· A blocker — they accelerate, not gate
· Responsible for tool access or licensing
· A replacement for the central AI program
Champion Responsibilities
· Own the prompt library for their role
· Run chain retros quarterly
· Submit ROI data to AVP monthly
· Onboard new team members to the chain
· Escalate enterprise blockers (tool access, policy)
Champion Selection Criteria
· Intrinsic curiosity about AI — not assigned by default
· Strong in their functional role first
· Communicates clearly across roles
· One champion per role minimum (BSA, Dev, QA, Prod)
