- Scripts, game ideas, troubleshooting, iteration

**Talk Track:**

## Slide 5 — What is an AI Champion?

**Definition:**

An AI Champion is an embedded advocate within a team who helps drive practical and responsible AI adoption.

**They:**
- Surface use cases  
- Help colleagues use approved tools and reusable assets  
- Share what’s working  
- Feed insights and blockers back into the program  

**Talk Track:**
At the simplest level, an AI Champion is someone inside the team who helps make AI real.

They’re not just promoting AI — they’re helping people actually use it in meaningful ways.

They connect strategy to day-to-day work.

They are change agents inside the team.

---

## Slide 6 — What AI Champions are working on

- **Advocate**
  - Promote approved AI tools  

- **Enable & Connect**
  - Help teams use tools, prompts, patterns  

- **Surface Use Cases & Blockers**
  - Identify where AI helps and what’s not working  

- **Represent & Feedback**
  - Bring insights back to the broader program  

**Talk Track:**
In practice, AI Champions do four things:

They advocate, enable, surface opportunities, and feed back into the system.

They help teams not just experiment — but learn and scale what works.
